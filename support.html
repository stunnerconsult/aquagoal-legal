Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a drink?

Tap the large "+" button on the Home screen. Choose a drink type (water, coffee, tea, juice, etc.), select an amount, and confirm. Your daily total updates immediately.

How do I change my daily hydration goal?

Open Settings → Daily Goal and adjust the value. You can also re-run onboarding from Settings to recalculate based on your weight and activity level.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not directly by AquaGoal. To cancel:

  1. Open the iOS Settings app on your device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select AquaGoal
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Your premium access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your AquaGoal purchase, and request a refund. Apple will review the request and notify you of their decision.

How do I restore purchases on a new device?

Open AquaGoal on the new device, go to Settings → Restore Purchases, or tap "Restore" on the paywall. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Your subscription will be restored.

Why isn't HealthKit syncing my hydration data?

Check that HealthKit permissions are enabled:

  1. Open the iOS Settings app
  2. Scroll to Health → Data Access & Devices → AquaGoal
  3. Make sure Water is enabled for both reading and writing

If permissions look correct, try toggling them off and back on, then restart AquaGoal.

I'm not getting reminder notifications. Why?

Check the following:

  • In iOS Settings → Notifications → AquaGoal, ensure notifications are enabled
  • In AquaGoal Settings → Notifications, ensure reminders are turned on and times are set
  • Make sure your device is not in Do Not Disturb or Focus mode that silences AquaGoal
  • Force-close and reopen the app to refresh scheduled notifications

How do I export my hydration history?

Go to Settings → Export Data. AquaGoal generates a CSV file you can share via email, AirDrop, Files, or any other supported app on your device.

Will I lose my data if I delete and reinstall the app?

Yes — AquaGoal stores data locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all logs, achievements, and settings. We recommend exporting your data (CSV) before uninstalling. HealthKit data you wrote stays in Apple Health.

Does AquaGoal work without an internet connection?

Yes. All core features work offline. An internet connection is only needed for the initial subscription purchase and StoreKit receipt validation.

What devices does AquaGoal support?

AquaGoal requires iOS 16.4 or later. It runs on iPhone. An Apple Watch companion app is not currently available.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. AquaGoal does not collect or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
