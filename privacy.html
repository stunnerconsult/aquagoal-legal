Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AquaGoal ("the app," "we," "our") handles your information when you use our iOS application. Your privacy is important to us. AquaGoal is designed to keep your data on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

AquaGoal does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. We do not operate any backend service that receives your data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Your location
  • Analytics, usage tracking, or crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Device identifiers

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Hydration intake logs (amount, drink type, timestamp)
  • Daily and weekly hydration goals
  • Achievements you have unlocked
  • App settings and preferences
  • Notification preferences and schedules

This data is stored using standard iOS storage and is included in your device backups (iCloud or local) if you have enabled them in iOS Settings. Those backups are managed by Apple, not by us.

3. HealthKit Integration

If you grant AquaGoal permission to access Apple HealthKit, the app may read and write hydration data (Dietary Water) to your Health app. This data is managed entirely by Apple's HealthKit framework and remains on your device or in your iCloud Health data, depending on your iOS settings.

We do not access, transmit, or store HealthKit data on any external server. AquaGoal will never use HealthKit data for advertising or share it with third parties.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

AquaGoal offers in-app subscriptions (weekly and yearly) through Apple's StoreKit. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Subscription status is verified through Apple's servers using StoreKit 2 receipts.

For information on how Apple handles your purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Notifications

If you enable notifications, hydration reminders are scheduled locally on your device. We do not send push notifications from any server, and no notification data is transmitted off-device.

6. Data Export

AquaGoal allows you to export your hydration history as a CSV file. This export is generated locally on your device. Where the file goes after you export it (email, Files app, AirDrop, etc.) is entirely your choice and governed by the privacy practices of whichever app or service you share it with.

7. Children's Privacy

AquaGoal does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we do not collect data, no special children's-data handling is required.

8. Your Rights

Because all your data is stored on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is visible inside the app at any time.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling AquaGoal removes all locally stored app data from your device. HealthKit data you wrote is managed in the Health app, where you can delete it directly.
  • Portability: Use the in-app CSV export to take your data with you.

9. Third-Party Services

AquaGoal uses the following Apple-provided frameworks: HealthKit, StoreKit, UserNotifications. We do not use any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated through the app or its App Store listing.
